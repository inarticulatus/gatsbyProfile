/*About Me*/

Currently, I am a data science enthusiast with interests in the machines, autonomous vehicles (I realize they are _machines_) and applications of AI. A particularly interesting topic that I stumbled across recently is the ethics of machines in general, and AI in particular. I absolutely love looking at data visualisations. Probalby me just wanting to create beautiful visualisations is partly the reason I am pursuing Data Science and the allied fields. However, the technological aids that data science provides has been inspiring to say the least.

I received my Bachelor's degree in Transportation Technology from National Rail and Transportation Institute in 2021. My undergrad thesis topic was Generalized ANN assisted data-driven aerodynamic optimization technique, where I explored the application of ANNs (or AI if you like) in the fields of aerodynamic shape optimization, which has long been plagued with high computation costs and high knowledge barrier.

As you can see, there are many things I am interested in, however, moving forward, I would like to do a Master's degree in fields relating to statistical learning and ethics.
